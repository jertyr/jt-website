TradesUP Chelsea

Working with a small team to bring a TradesUP-style trades showcase to Chelsea, Michigan. The goal is to create hands-on learning experiences where local kids can explore carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and other skilled trades alongside experienced professionals. These events shift perceptions about trade work and help young people discover fulfilling career paths through problem-solving and creativity.

If you're interested in supporting this effort or want to learn more about how you can help, please reach out.
